# Contributing to Autonomous Pharmacy Orchestrator

Thank you for being part of this groundbreaking journey in Ekiti land! We welcome all pharmacy students and tech enthusiasts to contribute.

## How to Contribute

1. **Suggest a Subtheme**: If you have a specific pharmacy problem in Nigeria that needs researching, add it to the `prompt.txt`.
2. **Improve PDF Formatting**: We are always looking for better ways to present our findings to clinicians.
3. **Refine AI Prompts**: Help us make the "Autonomous Pharmacy" vision more robust.

## Code of Conduct

As part of the PANS FUOYE community, we follow the principles of **Elegance et Excellence**.
- Be respectful to fellow participants.
- Focus on resilience and academic buoyancy.
- Reward excellence and collaboration.

## Technical Rules
- Use TypeScript for all logic.
- Ensure all new research features maintain HL7/FHIR compatibility.
- Test in low-bandwidth simulations.
