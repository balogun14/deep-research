RESEARCH OBJECTIVE:
Identify 3-5 groundbreaking, innovative projects that address the "last mile" of pharmacy in Nigeria. For each project, perform deep research into current local constraints and global AI benchmarks.

CORE CONSTRAINTS & WINNING CRITERIA:
1. NIGERIAN CONTEXT: Solutions must work with erratic power (offline-first), low bandwidth (edge-computing), and fragmented supply chains.
2. INTEROPERABILITY: Must explicitly mention HL7/FHIR compatibility and how to bridge data silos.
3. MEANINGFUL AI: Move beyond "chatbots." Use AI for predictive inventory, error detection, automated preparation, or post-market surveillance.
4. REDUCE COGNITIVE LOAD: The solution must free up the pharmacist's time (currently 70% admin).

1. PROBLEM ANALYSIS: Deep dive into why the current "last mile" in Nigeria is analog and fractured for this specific subtheme.
2. INNOVATIVE SOLUTION: A detailed "Autonomous Pharmacy" concept using specific AI models (e.g., Computer Vision for dispensing, Transformers for prescription verification).
3. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE: How it handles "Offline Mode" and "Low Bandwidth" while remaining HL7/FHIR compatible.
4. CLINICAL SAFETY: Explicitly describe how the AI flags risks and explains its reasoning (Explainable AI).
5. WINNING STRATEGY: A summary of why this specific idea will score high on Innovation, User Experience, and Technical Execution.

SUBTHEMES TO EXPLORE:
- Supply chain and Inventory (Visibility and Zero-Waste)
- Prescribing and Verification (Zero-Error)
- Preparation and Dispensing (Manual task automation)
- Administration and Adherence (Patient-centric last mile)
- Post Market Surveillance (Drug quality tracking)

TONE: 
Academic buoyancy, resilient, professional, and state-of-the-art. Focused on the transition from "Dangerous Analog" to "Seamless Self-Optimizing Ecosystem."
